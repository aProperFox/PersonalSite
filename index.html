Who is Tyler Olson?

Work related

I am a Software Engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Colorado at Boulder. I also have an interest in obtaining fluency in a foreign language and plan on living in a foreign country to further broaden my Spanish speaking ability.

In my freshman year at CU, I was drawn to mechanical engineering and pursued what potential careers it could bring. Along with three fellow students, I designed and created a glove to help complete everyday tasks that are difficult for citizens with a loss of ability in their hands. We donated the glove to one individual who was once an accomplished biker before a tragic accident. Despite his unfortunate past, he was ecstatic when we presented the glove to him and has been using it ever since. We were offered a patent on the design of the glove but regrettably declined due to a lack of funding. Recently, I built an Android virtual reality game with the intention of teaching users spanish vocabulary and the concept of language articles.

I'm most interested in programming user end content from websites to video games, but I try to work with everything in between. One day I aspire to own a business creating cross-platform applications that can make everyday tasks simpler to all users.
